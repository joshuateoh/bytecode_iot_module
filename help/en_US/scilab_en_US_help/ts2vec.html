ts2vec

Convert timestamp in milliseconds to time vector, with GMT offset

Syntax

timevec = ts2vec(timestamp,gmt)

Parameters

timestamp :

Time stamp in unix time milliseconds

gmt :

GMT offset to get the local time

timevec :

Time (Date) vector.

Description

Convert timestamp in milliseconds to time vector, with GMT offset.

See also

  • ts2sci — Convert timestamp in milliseconds to serial date number, with GMT offset
  • ts2str — Convert timestamp in milliseconds to human readable format, with GMT offset
  • vec2ts — Convert time vector to timestamp in milliseconds, with GMT offset
  • sci2ts — Convert serial date number to timestamp in milliseconds, with GMT offset
  • str2ts — Convert human readable format date to timestamp in milliseconds, with GMT offset
